# Privacy Policy for Tech Support Bot

**Last Updated: January 06, 2026**

## 1. Overview
The Tech Support Bot ("the App") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of **Anita Methodist School** staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data when you use the App within Google Chat.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following information to facilitate IT support services:

### A. User Information
- **Google Account Data:** Your name and email address (retrieved automatically to identify you as a staff member).
- **Chat ID:** A unique identifier for your Google Chat user to route messages.

### B. Usage Data
- **Support Tickets:** Any information you provide in a ticket, including descriptions, categories, priority levels, and location data.
- **Photos:** Images you upload to demonstrate technical issues.
- **Commands:** Interaction logs of commands used (e.g., `/ticket`, `/status`).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Your data is used strictly for internal operational purposes:
- **Ticket Management:** To create, track, and resolve your IT support requests.
- **Communication:** To send you status updates via Google Chat and Email.
- **AI Analysis:** Ticket descriptions and photos are processed by Google Gemini AI to:
  - Auto-categorize issues.
  - Suggest troubleshooting steps.
  - Extract text from error screenshots.
- **Scheduling:** To manage support staff rosters and leave requests.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
- **Storage Location:** All data is stored securely within the Organization's **Google Drive (Google Sheets)** and logging systems.
- **Access Control:** Data is accessible only to authorized IT support staff and administrators of Anita Methodist School.
- **No Third-Party Sales:** We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties.

## 5. Technology Partners
The App relies on services provided by Google Workspace. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data is handled in accordance with the Organization's existing Google Workspace agreement.

## 6. Data Access and Deletion
You have the right to request:
- Access to the historical data we hold about your tickets.
- Correction of inaccurate data.
- Deletion of specific data (subject to administrative record-keeping requirements).

To exercise these rights, please contact the ICT Department.
